Coleslaw

This sweet coleslaw recipe tastes like the slaw served at popular fried chicken or fish restaurants.

It's excellent with burgers or on top of BBQ pork sandwiches, too!

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 2/3 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whipâ„¢)
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  1. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.

  2. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar,

    poppy seeds, and salt together in a medium bowl until blended.

    Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat.

  3. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
